About The Event

A sacred gathering dedicated to the teachings of Paramahansa Yogananda – for seekers on the path of Self-Realization.

Paramahansa Yogananda

Paramahansa Yogananda (1893–1952) is widely regarded as the Father of Yoga in the West. He came to America in 1920 and spent decades teaching the ancient science of Kriya Yoga – a powerful meditation technique that accelerates spiritual development and brings the practitioner to the direct perception of God.

His landmark book, Autobiography of a Yogi, has inspired millions worldwide and continues to transform lives. Yogoda Satsanga Society of India (YSS), founded by him in 1917, carries forward his mission of spreading peace and spiritual wisdom.

Program Purpose

This 3-Day Spiritual Program at Anantapur is designed to provide an immersive retreat experience for devotees and seekers. Through meditation, discourse, bhajans, and selfless service, participants will deepen their spiritual practice and connect with a community of like-minded souls.

About Kriya Yoga

Kriya Yoga is an advanced Raja Yoga technique that reinforces and revitalizes subtle currents of life energy in the body. Regular practice leads to expanded awareness, inner peace, and ultimately union with the Divine. This program offers guidance on deepening one's Kriya practice.
